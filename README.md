# Your AI Learning Plan

A self-guided AI learning plan for professionals. Choose a 30-day, 3-month, or 6-month path, then build real skill through hands-on activities, practice drills, and curated courses. Track your progress as you go and apply AI to the work you already do.

**Open the tool:** https://clarifiandco.github.io/ai-learning-plan/

## What it is

An interactive, self-paced program that helps professionals get comfortable using AI and apply it to real work. It is not a technical course, and it needs no prior background.

## Who it is for

Any working professional who wants to build practical, applied AI capability, at whatever pace fits their goals.

## What is inside

- Three plans that build on each other: 30 days, 3 months, and 6 months
- Specific weekly activities that use your own real work
- A practice drill in every module
- Progress you can check off, saved on your device
- A curated course library spanning free, low-cost, and executive options

## How to use it

Open the link, choose the plan that fits your commitment, and work through it one session at a time. Check items off as you go. Your progress saves automatically in your browser.

## How to share it

Send the link to anyone. It opens in any browser on desktop or phone, works offline once loaded, and each person keeps their own progress.
